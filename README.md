The **Smart Traffic Signal System** is an IoT-based traffic management solution designed to reduce traffic congestion and improve road safety. It monitors real-time traffic density using sensors or cameras and automatically adjusts traffic signal timings based on vehicle flow. The system gives priority to emergency vehicles, minimizes unnecessary waiting time, and enhances overall traffic efficiency. By automating traffic control, it reduces fuel consumption, travel time, and air pollution while providing a smarter and more reliable transportation system for modern cities.
